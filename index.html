Admission Information

Maa Saraswati Shikshayatan offers academic guidance for students from Class 9 to Class 12. We specialize in the Arts stream, focusing on subjects such as:

  • Bengali
  • Education
  • History
  • Geography
  • Political Science
  • Philosophy

Admission Process:
Students or guardians can visit the institute during working hours for queries and enrollment. Necessary documents include:
  • Previous class marksheet
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • School ID card (if any)
For more details, feel free to contact us or visit our campus directly.
