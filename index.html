Admission

At Maa Saraswati Shikshayatan, we offer comprehensive coaching from Class VI through XII, covering all major subjects including Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Languages.

To apply for admission:

  • Download and complete the online admission form.
  • Submit the filled form along with photocopies of previous academic records.
  • Visit our center for a brief counseling session with our academic coordinator.

Seats are limited, so early application is advised!
